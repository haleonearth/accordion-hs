Models

The Binder Lift is available in two models – Nylon and Vinyl. Both models are proudly made in the USA and are covered by a 5-year warranty. The Vinyl model is made completely from medical-grade impervious materials, so decontamination and cleaning is quick and easy. Decontaminating and cleaning the Nylon model requires a machine wash and low-temperature drying.

NYLON

Sizes:

Standard: Chest range 34–62 inches and weight range of approx. 90–350 pounds.

Bariatric: Chest range 58–82 inches and weight range of approx. 350–800 pounds.

Material:

Torso Wrap: Denier 1000 Cordura Nylon
Handles: 2-inch polypropylene web strap

Leg Straps: 2-inch thermoplastic polyurethane slick-strap (100% impervious to fluids)

Weight and Storage Bag Dimensions:

Standard: Weight: 2.8 lbs Dimensions: 16" x 13"
Bariatric: Weight: 3.4 lbs Dimensions: 16" x 13"

VINYL

Sizes:

Standard: Chest range 34–62 inches and weight range of approx. 90–350 pounds.

Bariatric: Chest range 58–82 inches and weight range of approx. 350–800 pounds.

Material:

Torso Wrap: BondCote Vinyl (100% impervious to fluids)

Handles: 1.5-inch thermoplastic polyurethane slick-strap (100% impervious to fluids)

Leg Straps: 2-inch thermoplastic polyurethane slick-strap (100% impervious to fluids)

Weight and Storage Bag Dimensions:

Standard: Weight: 4 lbs Dimensions: 24" x 16"

Bariatric: Weight: 5.4 lbs Dimensions: 24" x 17"
